Immersive Distance Learning Platform for Electrical Apprentices

A cloud‑deployed Moodle + VR training ecosystem enabling apprentices to safely master high‑risk electrical procedures remotely.

Vision & Context

Electrical apprentices will go on to work on power grids, domestic circuits, and industrial systems. Mistakes in safety isolation, PPE usage, or tool selection can result in fatal consequences.

The goal was to redesign Health & Safety training so apprentices could learn theory remotely and repeatedly practice high‑risk procedures in a safe immersive environment before handling real equipment.

This elevates the project beyond “cool VR”.

The Core Problem

Training Limitations Before
  • Apprentices had to be physically present at TDR for theory and practical training
  • High-risk procedures (e.g., safety isolation) could only be practiced under supervision
  • Limited repetition opportunities
  • Inconsistent exposure to hazards
  • Resource and scheduling constraints
Why This Was a Problem
  • Safety-critical industry
  • Limited hands-on practice before real equipment exposure
  • Scalability constraints for distance learners
Why VR + LMS?
  • Moodle for structured theory delivery
  • VR for safe, repeatable, immersive practice
  • Cloud deployment for remote accessibility

Solution Architecture

Distance Learning Flow

  1. Complete Moodle theory modules
  2. Watch AI-generated training videos
  3. Enter VR scenarios
  4. Apply knowledge in immersive simulations
  5. Receive feedback and reinforcement

Technical Architecture

  • Moodle (LMS) hosted on Cloud VPS
  • Apache + PostgreSQL backend
  • Unity VR Application (OpenXR)
  • XR Interaction Toolkit
  • Blender assets
  • C# interaction logic

The VR Scenarios

Scenario 1: Hazard Identification

Learners explore a virtual workshop and identify safety hazards.

  • Interactive environment scanning
  • Contextual explanations upon selection
  • Educational feedback on risks and mitigation
  • Designed to improve environmental awareness

Learning Impact: Encourages attention to detail and proactive hazard recognition.

Scenario 2: Appropriate PPE Selection

Learners equip a worker with correct PPE for workshop conditions.

  • Interactive object pickup
  • On-selection text guidance
  • Voice-over explanations
  • Reinforced safety rationale

Learning Impact: Strengthens procedural memory for PPE compliance.

Scenario 3: Safety Isolation (Critical Scenario)

High-risk electrical isolation procedure simulated in VR.

  • Step-by-step isolation process
  • Consequence awareness
  • Repeatable practice
  • Tutor-validated workflow design

Why This Matters: Incorrect isolation in real life can cause fatal injuries. VR allows unlimited safe repetition before real-world execution.

Scenario 4: Tools Selection

Learners select correct tools for electrical circuit work.

  • Equipment evaluation
  • Error-based learning
  • Reinforced safe tool usage

Impact: Reduces risk of short circuits, equipment damage, and injury.
